Structural Tags

Header

The header is the same in every page. It usually contains their brand or name on the header as well as information that
wont change as you change to different webpages of your website.

NAV

Links go into the navigation. Sometimes the navitagation will be in the header. It does not have to be in the header.
The navigation does not contain links to another webpage other than your own.

MAIN

The main tag is used where you want to put the main part of the webpage for each different webpage for your website.
The MAIN tag has different information inside of the tag for each web page.

FOOTER

The footer is located at the bottom of page, where they contain legally required information. They also contain copy right informatiion and may contain links to other websites such as social media.

HTML Validator

The HTML Validator is used to find errors or mistakes that you might have made inside your webpage. Using HTML Validator is a good coding habit that people should remember after they finish their coding.
