Husband/Father of Forger's

Loid Forger

Loid is a generic cinematic spy. He works for Wise and is lives a double life. Where in one life, he is a agent codenamed "Twilight". The other life, he is a husband and a father. Not much is known about his past other than that he was an orphan of war.

Wife/Mother of Forger's

Yor Forger

Yor Forger is a assassin working for the Garden. For those who know her true identity, she is known as the thorn princess. She is a caring mother of Anya and would do whatever it takes to protect them. She works for Ostania and has a younger brother.

Daughter of Forger's

Anya Forger

Anya may seem like a ordinary child, she has telepathy, in which she can read people's thoughts. Although she is a very kind and caring child, who loves to play with her friends, she is also cunning, using her powers, she can be a bit mischievous.
