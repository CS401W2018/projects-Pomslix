FPS Games

FPS games are games that usually have players split into two teams and will have objectives that players work towards. The games that I have chosen are Call of Duty, PUBG and Counter Strike.

Survival Games

Survival Games are games where you are put into world and try to survive throughout the days and see how long you can last for. The games that I have chosen are Minecraft, Terraria, and Rust.

Gacha Games

Gacha games are games that usually require you to spend in game currency's to obtain in game items. You can usually buy in game currency's using real life money. The games that I have chosen are Genshin Impact, Wuthering Waves, and Fate/Grand Order.

Horror Games

Horror games are games that try to make the player scared by inducing fear, anxiety into them. They are done usually by jumpscares, music, and the atmosphere of the game. Games that I have chosen are Phasmophobia, Dead by Daylight, and Resident Evil.

MMORPG Games

MMORPG games are game that have a massive multiplayer that usually has in game currency that players can earn to trade with other. They usually have dungeons and pvp that players can partake in. Games that I have chosen are FFXIV, WoW, and RuneScape.
