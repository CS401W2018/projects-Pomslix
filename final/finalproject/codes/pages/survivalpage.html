Survival Page

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is owned by Activision which was founded in 1979, The made games like Call of Duty and and Tony Hawk's Pro Skater. Today, the Call of Duty franchise remains one of the top most played games today, with hundres of thousand of players active. It is a players vs players game usually consisting of 5v5 with different game mode such as TDM, FFA, Dominations.

source: Britannica

PUBG

PUBG is a battle royale game where players can team up together up to a 4 stack and fight with other players to try to remain the last person or party standing. The match usually has 100 players per game and people will start off from a airplane and drop to locations to find weapons, armor, ammunition to survive.

source: Wikipedia

Counter Strike

Counter Strike is a competitive multiplayer where 5 players goes against other 5 players. The game is centered around players either trying to prevent the other team from planting and detonating a bomb. Or trying to plant the bomb and preventing the other team from defusing the bomb. It has a massive following and is made by Valve.

source: Wikipedia
